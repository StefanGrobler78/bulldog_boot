About me

My name is Lelanie and I am a devoted breeder of exotic and standard French Bulldogs since 2017. I am situated in a small town called Petrus Steyn, but have many Frenchie family members all over South Africa.

I love my puppies from the moment they are born and provide lifelong continuous support to their pawrents after the puppies leave to their new homes.

Frenchies have a one of a kind personality, guaranteed to make you smile.

If you are interested in adopting one of my special Frenchie family members, please feel free to contact me.

The price depends on the colour, age, gender, markings and the discretion of the breeder. Girls cost more than boys. You can expect the price to be anywhere between R20000 and R50000.

You will come across cheaper options, and be tempted by these low prices, but please note that there are many scammers out there as well as unethical breeders who breed just to make a profit.

Frenchies are difficult to breed with, and require special methods and practices like artificial insemination, c-sections, and full time care after being borne. Once the puppies get bigger they need to checked by a vet regularly, they require vaccinations, dewormers, and other treatments. The puppies also require the best food, a safe place to play and sleep, and good quality toys.

My puppies are from good bloodlines, some of our adults are imported from other countries, and we are also registered with kennel clubs.

All my puppies go home with a generous puppy pack, the content of the pack varies from litter to litter.

As you can see there is much time, effort, care and money that goes into breeding with French Bulldogs to ensure you receive a healthy, happy, family member.
